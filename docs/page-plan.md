# Treda Städ - Full Site Page Plan

## Business Goal
**Primary:** Get Quote Requests / Bookings (Få Offert)
**Secondary:** Build trust for long-term contracts (Företag)

## Target Audience
- **B2C:** Homeowners in Malmö seeking reliable, premium help.
- **B2B:** Office managers/Business owners needing consistent cleaning.
- **Tone:** "Warm Corporate" – Trustworthy, Professional, yet Approachable.

## Site Structure & Section Flows

### 1. Home Page / (Existing - Refine)
- **Goal:** Establish trust immediately & direct to booking.
- **Hero:** "Rent hem, utan stress" + Pill CTA.
- **Social Proof:** "500+ nöjda kunder i Malmö".
- **Services Teaser:** Top 3 (Hem, Stor, Flytt) cards.
- **Value Prop:** "Varför Treda?" (Security, Guarantee, Time).
- **CTA:** Dark section "Redo för ett renare hem?".

### 2. Tjänster Page /tjanster (Existing - Refine)
- **Goal:** Show breadth of offering.
- **Hero:** Simple "Våra Tjänster".
- **Grid:** 6 Cards (Hem, Stor, Flytt, Bygg, Kontor, Fönster).
- **Detail:** Each card lists 3 key features (e.g., "Fönsterputs ingår").
- **CTA:** "Hittar du inte det du söker?" -> Contact.

### 3. Företag (Business) /foretag
- **Goal:** Convince businesses to switch to Treda.
- **Hero:** "Rent kontor, bättre fokus."
- **Focus:** Reliability, Custom Schedules, Invoice handling.
- **Trust:** "Ansvarsförsäkring" & "Kollektivavtal" (if applicable/generic trust).
- **CTA:** "Få Företagsoffert" (Different form/intent).

### 4. Om Oss (About) /om-oss
- **Goal:** Humanize the company.
- **Content:** The story of Treda. Focus on "Quality over Quantity".
- **Team:** Pictures of key staff or generic friendly cleaners (Leasio style).
- **Values:** "Trygghet", "Kvalitet", "Miljö".
- **CTA:** "Bli en av oss" (Recruitment) or "Boka oss".

### 5. Kontakt / Offert /kontakt
- **Goal:** Capture the lead.
- **Layout:** Split screen.
    - **Left:** Contact info (Phone, Email, Malmö Address).
    - **Right:** Simple multi-step form (Type of cleaning -> Sq meters -> Contact info).

### 6. Legal / Privacy
- **Content:** Standard GDPR, Cookies.

## Design DNA Check (Leasio)
- **Typography:** Manrope throughout.
- **Colors:** Warm Beige (`#F5F4EF`) background, Deep Navy Text.
- **Shapes:** Pill buttons, `8px` radius cards, `shadow-leasio`.
- **Spacing:** Generous (`py-24` or `py-32`).

## Validation
✓ Does the Hero hook the user? Yes ("Rent hem").
✓ Is the conversion path clear? Yes (Pill Buttons everywhere).
✓ Are B2B and B2C needs addressed? Yes (Separate /foretag page).
